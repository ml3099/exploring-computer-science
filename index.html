Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a Netflix origional show about our world vs another dimention that is exactly like our world. This show is based in the 80's The story follows five main characters named Mike, Lucas, Dustin, Will, and 11. 11 is from a secret lab where she learned how to do telekenesis. Will goes missing in the first episode and the four other boys go looking for him in the woods and find 11. The first season is based around these 5 characters trying to defeat a monster called the demogorgan from the other dimention called the upside down. The only way to get to the unpside down is to go through a gate that 11 originaly opened. In the end of the first season, 11 kills the demogorgan, but by doing so, she is sent to the upside down.
